Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2018-02-28

drag'n'drop uses Unity Analytics software to gather anonymous information about game usage and player behaviour. Types of information that are gathered:

  • Completed levels
  • Time needed to complete every level
  • Whether a level was abandoned or restarted

Additional information may by gathered as described in Unity Analytics's privacy policy that can be reviewed here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
